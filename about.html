Get More Done in Less Time

Connecting Taskers / Service Provider and Peoples to Out-Source Tasks

SAS Service Provider is a trusted platform that connects people who need to outsource tasks and find local services, with people who are looking to earn money and ready to work. From simple to complicated tasks, SAS can help you to complete your home cleaning, handyman jobs, home & office services, admin work, photography, graphic design or even build a website.

What do you need done?

Start by telling us about your task. Mention when and where (in person or online) you need it done, then suggest a fair budget for the task. Post any task you need from cleaning to web design in only two minutes – for free! There's no obligation to hire.

Task completed

With your task complete, you just need to release the payment held with Seekkr Escrow. Then you’re free to leave a review for the Tasker / Service Provider so everyone can know what a great job they’ve done!

Choose the best person for you

Take a look at profiles and reviews to pick the best Tasker / Service Provider for your task. When you accept an offer, your payment is held securely with SAS Escrow until the task is complete. Now you can message and call the Tasker / Service Provider to sort out the details
